# Outer Ring Territories

These territories magnify, redirect, or conceal instability originating
from the core.

They rarely collapse first.
They adapt — sometimes too well.

---

## Commons (Mediators + Junctions)
Public gathering spaces.
Bars, halls, shared grounds.

Function:
- visibility
- rumor
- soft social enforcement

Failure mode:
Silence becomes the default response to wrongdoing.

---

## Junctions (Crafts + Mediators)
Crossroads, inns, staging areas.

Function:
- temporary coordination
- hiring
- information exchange

Failure mode:
Utility replaces responsibility.
People pass through without consequence.

---

## Markets (Mediators + Resources)
Exchange and valuation.

Function:
- fairness perception
- negotiation
- redistribution

Failure mode:
Value detaches from labor.
“Everyone benefits” becomes untestable.

---

## Fields (Resources)
Cultivation, herding, managed abundance.

Function:
- stability through cycles
- visible cost of waste

Failure mode:
Loss is felt but not traced.
Blame diffuses outward.

---

## Foragers (Resources + Margins)
Wild harvesters, scavengers.

Function:
- flexible supply
- recovery of discarded value

Failure mode:
Ethics blur between found and taken.

---

## Wastes (Margins)
Sparse, semi-livable zones.

Function:
- disappearance
- avoidance

Failure mode:
People vanish without being missed.

---

## Relics (Margins + Archives)
Old sites, remnants, taboo caches.

Function:
- memory without custody

Failure mode:
History exists without guardianship.

---

## Schools (Archives)
Training, rites, apprenticeships.

Function:
- converting memory into behavior

Failure mode:
Teaching persists after relevance fades.

---

## Couriers (Archives + Banks)
Trusted carriers, sealed transfers.

Function:
- movement of meaning and value

Failure mode:
Trust is assumed longer than deserved.

---

## Mints (Banks)
Valuation, tokens, guarantees.

Function:
- abstraction of worth

Failure mode:
Worth outpaces reality.

---

## Quarries (Banks + Crafts)
Raw materials.

Function:
- physical foundation of production

Failure mode:
Extraction accelerates without oversight.

---

## Forges (Crafts)
High-output creation.

Function:
- pride
- danger
- transformation

Failure mode:
Skill becomes status.
Accidents become acceptable.
