# Arc 01 — Getting Lost

## Function of this arc
- Establish displacement as systemic rather than personal failure.
- Introduce belonging as something that can be interrupted externally.

## Internal shift
- The child learns that origin is not guaranteed.
- Loss occurs before identity fully forms.

## Structural notes
- The planet’s action is not framed as malicious.
- Adults around the event may normalize it, ritualize it, or avoid explaining it.

## What this arc is not
- Not a revenge origin.
- Not a prophecy or calling.
- Not the beginning of heroism.
