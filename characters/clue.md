# Clue

## Status
- Female
- Integrated member of Mediators
- Low-status among females
- High observational credibility

## Origin
- Relocated by the planet years earlier
- Memories partially blurred
- Retained tri-color fur, indicating successful integration

## Physical Traits
- Listening-adapted tail (fine-motor, sensory-biased)
- Missing half of one ear
- The loss predates her memory clarity

## Name
"Clue" is not a birth name.

Others began using it because:
- She notices what others miss
- She listens without interrupting
- She rarely speaks unless something matters

She kept it.

## Core Tension
Clue sees moral drift in her tribe.
She stays silent to prevent abuse of leniency.
This silence costs her credibility and intimacy.

She does not follow the Wanderer to keep him.
She follows him to confirm something about herself.
