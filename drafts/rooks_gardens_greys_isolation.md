# Rooks, Gardens, Greys, and Isolation (Draft)

## Gardens (borders)
- Borders between tribes are called “gardens”.
- Rooks are physically restricted to gardens.
- They may traverse the planet only via connected gardens.
- Movement is possible as long as borders still connect living tribes.

## Delegation
- Rooks may delegate observation and maintenance to other rooks.
- Responsibility is shared, but proximity still matters when systems fail.

## Collapse condition
- If two neighboring tribes go empty, the border between them loses meaning.
- A meaningless border cannot justify a rook’s continued freedom.

## Greys
- Greys are system enforcers, not moral agents.
- When a border becomes meaningless, greys locate the associated rook.
- The rook is locked between the empty borders.
