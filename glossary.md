# Glossary (Selective)

This glossary explains terms only when ambiguity becomes harmful.

---

**Getting Lost**  
A planetary mechanism relocating adult litras to new territories.
Not punishment. Not reward.

**Mono-colored**  
A state indicating interrupted tribal continuity.

**Gardens**  
Border paths maintained by rooks.

**Greys**  
Planetary enforcement entities. Rare. Final.

**Social Deference**  
A non-verbal protocol indicating submission without loss of dignity.

**Empty Territory**  
A systemic anomaly. Always dangerous.

---

This glossary is intentionally incomplete.
